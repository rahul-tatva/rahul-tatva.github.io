How to style a suit, according to Margot Robbie

Angelica Xidias24 June 2021

The homegrown star makes the case for stylish suiting.

 
1/15

Image credit: Getty Images

Vogue Australia may receive advertising or affiliate commission if you buy through our links. Read more here. 

Theres no denying that Margot Robbie has an affinity for stylish suiting. The trend, which is inarguably less of a fleeting fad and more of a wardrobe mainstay, is one the homegrown talent has subscribed to for quite some time. And with the help of stylist to the stars Kate Young, shes proven time and time again that there are countless ways to style the beloved staple.

One such way which challenges the very nature of the typically formal style is by dressing it down with a set of leggings. Robbie did just that at a screening of Mary Queen of Scots in 2018, again during Paris Fashion Week in 2019, and most recently while promoting Suicide Squad 2 in Los Angeles. For this occasion, she stepped out in a bright red set designed by Italian label, The Andamane.

Personally, I love beautiful things but Im also pragmatic: I look for freedom of movement, the actor revealed in a 2019 interview on the topic of her approach to fashion. I love a silk suit, flats and the mix of masculine and feminine style. That makes me feel powerful and feminine, comfortable but confident. I think nothings more alluring than confidence.

Balancing out an oversized boxy blazer with bare legs is also another way Robbie has notably schooled us on styling the adaptable wardrobe staple. Whether you decide to opt for a blazer dress sans bottoms, or choose to pair a shorter iteration with a mini skirt, youre guaranteed to step out in a winning ensemble when taking a leaf out of the actors style playbook. 

Here, Vogue charts all the artful ways to wear a suit according to Robbie, and where to shop each style.
